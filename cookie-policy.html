Cookie Policy - Gaming News and Entertainment

On our website, dedicated to all things gaming, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. To enhance the experience of our visitors and players, we use cookies on our website. This Cookie Policy aims to explain what cookies are, how we use them, the types of cookies we employ, and how you can manage your cookie settings.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or other internet-enabled devices) when you visit a website. These files contain information that allows the website to remember your activities or preferences over time. Cookies are commonly used to improve the functionality of websites, make them more efficient, and offer a personalized user experience.

There are different types of cookies used for various purposes. Some cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly, while others are used to track user behavior for website improvement or advertising purposes.

2. Why Do We Use Cookies on Our Website?

On our gaming-focused website, we use cookies for several reasons. Here are some of the primary purposes for which we employ cookies:

  • Enhancing User Experience: Cookies help us store your preferences and settings, so you don’t need to re-enter the same information every time you visit our site.
  • Personalization: Cookies enable us to provide you with content and ads based on your behavior on our site, offering you a personalized gaming experience.
  • Analysis and Performance: We use cookies to gather data on how users interact with our website, helping us improve functionality and design.
  • Advertising: We use cookies to display relevant ads related to gaming, pokies, online games, and other entertainment content.
  • Security: Some cookies are used to ensure the security of our website by verifying user identity and protecting against fraud.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

There are different categories of cookies used on our website. Below, we describe the main categories of cookies we use:

3.1. Necessary Cookies

Necessary cookies are essential for the proper functioning of our website. Without these cookies, certain parts of the site may not be accessible or function correctly. These cookies do not require consent and are activated automatically when you visit our site.

Examples of necessary cookies we use:

  • Session Cookies: These cookies allow the website to remember your activities during your browsing session, such as keeping you logged in as you navigate between pages.
  • Security Cookies: We use cookies to verify that users are authorized to access certain areas of the website, such as their personal account or contact forms.

3.2. Performance Cookies

Performance cookies allow us to analyze how users interact with our website. This data helps us improve the performance of the site and understand which areas need enhancement. Performance cookies collect information anonymously and are not linked to personal data.

Examples of performance cookies we use:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics cookies to gather data about website traffic and user behavior, such as the most visited pages and time spent. This information helps us optimize the user experience.

3.3. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies enable the website to remember your selections and preferences, such as language, region, or gaming categories you’re interested in. These cookies improve the personalization of your experience on our website.

Examples of functional cookies we use:

  • Language Preferences: We use cookies to store your preferred language so you don’t need to select it every time you visit our site.
  • Search Preferences: We use cookies to remember the filters and criteria you’ve selected, such as types of online games, pokies, or specific gaming rewards, making your search more efficient.

3.4. Advertising Cookies

Advertising cookies are used to display personalized ads based on your browsing behavior. These cookies allow us to show you ads that are relevant to online gaming, pokies, and other entertainment content.

Examples of advertising cookies we use:

  • Google Ads: We use Google Ads cookies to show you personalized ads based on your activity on our website and your browsing behavior on other sites.
  • Facebook Pixel: We use Facebook Pixel cookies to display ads related to gaming and entertainment on our website and social media platforms.

3.5. Social Media Cookies

Social media cookies enable the integration of our website with social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and LinkedIn. These cookies allow you to share content directly from our website via social media buttons and measure user interactions with our profiles.

Examples of social media cookies we use:

  • Facebook: We use Facebook cookies to show personalized ads and allow users to share content on their Facebook profiles.
  • Twitter: We use Twitter cookies to share content on Twitter and track interactions with our Twitter profile.

4. How to Manage Cookies

You have complete control over the cookies stored on your device. You can disable or block cookies at any time by adjusting the settings of your browser. However, please note that disabling some cookies may affect certain features of our website.

Here’s how you can manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Go to "Settings" > "Privacy and Security" > "Site Settings" > "Cookies" and adjust your preferences.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Go to "Options" > "Privacy & Security" > "Cookies and Site Data" and adjust your preferences.
  • Safari: Go to "Settings" > "Privacy" and adjust cookie options.
  • Microsoft Edge: Go to "Settings" > "Privacy, Search, and Services" > "Cookies and Site Data" and adjust your preferences.

5. Links to Other Websites

This website may contain links to other websites that are not operated or controlled by us. This cookie policy applies only to our website. Therefore, we recommend that you check the cookie policy on any other website you visit.

6. Changes to the Cookie Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this cookie policy at any time. Any changes to our policy will be posted on this page. Therefore, we recommend checking this policy regularly.
