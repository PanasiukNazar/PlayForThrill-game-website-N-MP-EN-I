2. Collection of Personal Data

We collect and store all information you knowingly provide to us when you fill out online forms on the Website. You can choose not to provide certain information, but in this case, you may not be able to use some of the features of the Website.

3. Collection of Non-Personal Data

When you visit the Website, our servers automatically log the information your browser sends. This data may include information such as your device's IP address, browser type and version, operating system type and version, preferred language, and the page you visited before coming to our Website. The pages you visit on our Website, the time spent on those pages, the information you search for on our Website, and your access times and dates are also recorded.

All information we collect from you may be used to improve customer service and respond to inquiries and emails from our users; operate and manage the Website and Services. The non-personal data collected will only be used to identify potential misuse and create statistical information about the usage of the Website. These statistical data do not identify any specific user of the system.

5. Cookies

We may use cookies to collect, store, and track information to operate the Website and Services. You can decide whether to accept or reject cookies. Most web browsers accept cookies by default, but you can change your browser settings to reject them if you prefer.

Google uses the collected data to display ads that may interest you and may share this information with other Google services. You can disable Google ad personalization by visiting Google's ad settings. You can also choose not to receive personalized ads from Google by visiting the opt-out page of the Google advertising networks: https://optout.networkadvertising.org/.

7. Security of Your Information

We secure the information you provide to us on our servers in a controlled and secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical protective measures to safeguard against unauthorized access, use, modification, and disclosure of personal data under our control and supervision.

Our Website contains links to other websites that are not owned or controlled by us. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of these other websites or third parties. We encourage you to be aware when you leave our Website and to read the privacy policies of each website that collects personal data.
